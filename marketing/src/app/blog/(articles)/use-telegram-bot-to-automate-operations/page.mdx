### Step 1: Sign In to Your Account

To begin using our tailored Telegram bot, [sign in](https://credivio.vercel.app/sign-in) to your account on Credivio.

### Step 2: Obtain Your Telegram Token

After signing in, you'll need to obtain your Telegram Token. This token is necessary to connect the bot to your account. Navigate to the [Automations page](https://credivio.vercel.app/automations) and copy your prepared Telegram Bot Token.

### Step 3: Paste the Token into the Bot Chat

With your token in hand, the next step is to connect your account with the bot chat. To do this:

1. **Start**: Click the "Start" button on the bottom of [chat](https://web.telegram.org/k/#@CredivioBot),
2. **Register**: Paste and send your token to the bot

The bot will automatically verify your token assign and assign your account to it. From now on, you can use the bot to add operations!
